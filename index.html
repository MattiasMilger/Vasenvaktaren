Player Profile

Game Guide


Taming

Tame a Väsen by offering it its desired item, and then defeating it. Find out the desired item by carefully reading the description or asking the Väsen during combat.

Attributes

Strength = Damage with Strength attacks.
Wisdom = Damage with Wisdom attacks.
Defense = Reduction from Strength attacks.
Durability = Reduction from Wisdom attacks.

Runes

Equip runes to your Väsen for powerful effects. At lvl 30, they gain the ability to equip two runes.

Megin

Megin is the flowing inner force your väsen draw upon to act. Abilities aligned with their own element cost 15% less Megin, and they naturally restore 12% Megin each round.

Healing

You can heal your väsen outside of combat by finding a sacred well (80% heal) or giving them your items (50% heal for the wrong item and 80% heal for the right item). There is also a small after-combat heal of 5%.

Attribute Stages

Some abilities and runes raise or lower attribute stages. They Increase or decrease the affected attribute by 10% per stage, stages go from -5 to +5.

Move Priority

Swapping > Utility Moves > Attack moves. Player has priority.

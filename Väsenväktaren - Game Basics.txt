Attribute Stages
Some abilities and runes have stat stages.
Stat stages go from -5 to +5 and give 10% attribute changes (so maximum -50% or +50%) to a specific attribute. Once they reach the negative cap it cannot go any lower, and once it reaches the positive cap it cannot go any further. -5 and +5 are hard limits.
These do not persist after a battle ends but do persist in endless tower. Attribute stage changes do not include health, references to “reduce/raise all attributes” only means Defense, durability, strength and wisdom”. Attempting to raise an attribute already at +5 or lower one at -5 has no effect but still costs Megin.

Total Attribute Calculation:
((Attribute Stage Modifier) * ((Temperament Modifiers + 1 + Attribute Level Scaling Rate * (Level - 1)) * (Rarity Modifiers * (**Base Attributes + Element Modifiers**)))) Round Down Final Number



Attribute Level Scaling Rate = 0.035
Attribute Stage Modifier = 1 + ( 0.08 * [stage] )

Resources
Megin
Megin - Megin is a resource used to take actions, it is not an attribute and therefore not calculated with your attribute modifiers.
Abilities of your own element costs 12% less Megin. You regenerate 12% of your max Megin per round. When you are swapped out you still regenerate your Megin.

Megin calculation

Total Megin = Base Megin + ((Level - 1) * Megin Per Level).


Base Megin = 60
Megin Per Level = 2

Megin per round

Megin Per Round = Total Megin * Megin Regen Rate

Megin Regen Rate = 0.12

Megin recovers fully after each combat.

Insufficient Megin
When you have insufficient Megin abilities will simply be greyed out not allowing you to use them.

Health
Health persists between battles.


Ways to heal:
After each battle your active party is healed by 5% (even if their life is 0, it  will heal to 5%)
Outside of combat you can give taming items to your Väsen.
Wrong item: 50% heal
Right item: 80% heal
Sacred Well: When you encounter this,  all your Väsen drink and heal (even the ones not in the party) by 80%

All heal percentages are based on total/max health and not current.

Experience:
Experience needed for level-up:

( (Base Level Up Experience * Current Level) + (Level Up Acceleration  * Current Level²) ) Round Down Final Number

Experience received for defeating enemy:

( (Base Experience Yield * Enemy Level) * Rarity Bonus ) Round Down Final Number


Constants:

Base Level Up Experience = 55 (may be changed in development)

Level Up Acceleration = 5 (may be changed in development)

Base Experience Yield = 50 (may be changed in development)

The Väsen which deals the killing blow and ends the battle exclusively gains 100% of the experience. Väsen in the party which were in the field during the combat for at least a turn exclusively gain 70% of the experience. Väsen who were in the party but were not in the field for at least a turn exclusively get 50% of the experience. Väsen in the party who died/fainted do not get any experience at all.

Experience is calculated during and only gained AFTER combat, meaning tactics such as surrendering before combat ends does not yield any experience. Experience is tallied and applied only after combat ends. Mid-battle defeats do not trigger level-ups.


Damage
Damage Calculation:
((( Power / Power Constant ) Damage Range * Damage Modifier * Element Modifier * Rune Modifier) * ( 1 - (Defense Modifier / (Defense Modifier + Defense Constant )))) Round Down Final Number

Notes and explanations regarding damage calculation:
Power Constant = 250 (Used to convert the numbers that the abilities show to something that translates better to the overall damage calculation)
Defense Constant = 200 (Used to convert the defense value to something that translates better to the overall damage calculation)
Damage Range = a randomized value between 1 and 1.1 to simulate damage ranges
Damage Modifier = Strength/Wisdom
Element Modifier = Weak/Normal/potent
Rune Modifier Adds the percentage bonuses that your runes give. So for example if one gives 10% damage and one gives 20% damage, it gets calculated like: 1 + 0.1 + 0.2 = 1.3
Runes
Runes are equippable modifiers to your character. The väsen can only equip one rune from lvl 1 to 29, but gain the ability for two once reached max level (30). Enemy Väsen of all types will have one random rune (two at level 30 and above). You can see what runes you and they have as well as the effect via tooltip. 
Rune Exclusivity: Your own runes are exclusive (I.E. can only be wielded by one of your Väsen at a time, enemies don't count), and are put in slots in the active party (I.E. they are bound to a party slot). This rule must be obeyed by guardians as well who’s team can’t have duplicate runes (I.E. two ᚱ Raido runes). Starter rune is ᚢ Ur. Once you have a specific rune in your possession it will not show up again in exploration.
Rune requirement: Enemies MUST have one rune (lvl 1) and two runes (lvl 30).
As for the player, the rune slots must have a rune in them. When you find new runes while a spot is empty, it will be auto filled with the new rune. When you have all three rune spots filled, there is simply no possibility to have an empty rune.
